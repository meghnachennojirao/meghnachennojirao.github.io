Research

Current and previous research.

Master's Thesis

Effects of tprkb deficiency on the expression of neuronal transcription factors in zebrafish

Ongoing

Guide: Dr. Gunimala Chakraborty, HOD & Associate Professor, Dept. of Molecular Genetics & Cancer.

  • Working toward stable tprkb mutants using CRISPR-Cas9 with validation through HRMA and HD assays.
  • Assessing developmental phenotypes and neuronal transcription factor expression through WISH and RNA-based analysis.
  • Techniques include CRISPR-Cas9, HRMA, HD assay, developmental screening, WISH, and cDNA synthesis.

Bachelor's Dissertation

Assessment of treatment outcome in eczema with homoeopathic constitutional medicine using SCORAD

May 2019

Guide: Dr. Vilma D'souza, BHMS, MD(Hom), MS (Counselling & Psychotherapy).

  • Conducted a 25-patient case series evaluating pre- and post-treatment SCORAD scores.
  • Observed statistically significant reduction in SCORAD scores (p = 0.006), while noting no control group and small sample size.
