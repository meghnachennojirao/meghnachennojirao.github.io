ABOUT ME

With a commitment to the art of healing (and not the dark arts, I promise) and an oath to provide more than just band-aid solutions, I plunge into the depths of medicine, neuroscience, and the vast maze of the human psyche. Beyond the stethoscope, and occasionally the karaoke microphone, my soul dances to music's rhythms, and my voice conveys more than just medical wisdom. Whether penning poems, blogging about the vastness of life, delving into spiritual introspection, contemplating the cosmos, or marvelling at astronomical wonders, I might also be found trying to convince my plants to talk back or chasing after my mischievous pets with a piece of yarn. All the while, I remain immersed in the philosophies of spirituality. Here's to life, laughter, and an insatiable thirst for knowledge!
