About

Curiosity across molecules, memory, and meaning.

I have spent most of my life wondering, "Why did I do that?" I looked for answers in psychology, then neuroscience, molecular biology, and epigenetics. Over time, I realized it is not just the answers I enjoy; it is the pursuit itself.

Whether it is molecules, hormones, trauma, or spirituality, I am drawn to uncovering truths from every angle. I find joy not only in science, but also in literature, poetry, and the stories the cosmos tell.
