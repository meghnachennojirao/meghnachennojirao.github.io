Turiya Lab

Understanding Mind, Brain, and Behavior

Led by Dr. Meghna Rao, Turiya Lab investigates the intricate relationships between genes, molecules, cognition, and consciousness. Our integrative approach spans from genomics to behavior, combining molecular neuroscience, cognitive psychology, and consciousness studies to understand the human experience across multiple levels of analysis.
